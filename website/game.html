The Big Line Big Clique Conjecture

How to play

Click on the canvas to create a point at the spot you just clicked. You can only place points at the intersections of the squared lines. The goal of the game is to place the most possible points on the canvas with the least possible collinear points and/or pairwise visible points. This is referenced as the clique number and is the size of the maximal clique. The colors showed on the graph only work with graphs that have a chromatic number lower than 7, for chromatic numbers bigger than 7, the points will all be colored in red.

The way the chromatic number/coloration is computed is poorly implemented and takes a lot of time for big graphs, it is recommended to toggle it off if you want to try big graphs (graphs with over 30 vertices or clique number higher than 7 are considered "big" as the computation time for them starts to get noticeable).
